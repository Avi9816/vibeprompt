# \## SUMMARY

# 

# Kling AI prefers prompts written as concise cinematic visual direction with strong emphasis on subject clarity, action, camera movement, and realistic motion behavior. The guide repeatedly encourages prompts that explicitly describe how subjects move, how the camera behaves, and what cinematic style defines the scene. Motion realism, dynamic camera language, lighting, atmospheric effects, and high-detail visual specificity are consistently emphasized. The model performs best when prompts are visually grounded, motion-focused, stylistically coherent, and written with direct cinematic clarity rather than narrative abstraction.
