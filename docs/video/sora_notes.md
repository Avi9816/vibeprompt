# \## SUMMARY

# 

# Sora 2 prefers prompts written as visually explicit cinematic scene direction with strong emphasis on subject clarity, action sequencing, camera behavior, and temporal continuity. The guide repeatedly encourages prompts that describe exactly what happens on screen, how the camera moves, how the environment behaves, and how the scene evolves over time. Cinematic terminology, lighting detail, atmosphere, motion description, and grounded environmental specificity are consistently encouraged. The model performs best when prompts are clear, sequential, visually concrete, and focused on coherent scene progression rather than abstract storytelling alone.
