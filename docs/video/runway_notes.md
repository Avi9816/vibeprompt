# \## SUMMARY

# 

# Runway Gen-4 prefers prompts written as clear cinematic visual direction with strong emphasis on subject clarity, physical action, camera behavior, lighting, and scene consistency. The guide repeatedly encourages concise but visually dense prompting using filmmaking terminology, explicit motion descriptions, and grounded environmental detail. Prompts perform best when they describe exactly what should appear on screen, how the camera behaves, how the scene moves over time, and what cinematic style or atmosphere should define the shot. The guide consistently favors concrete visual specificity over abstract storytelling or overly verbose narrative writing.
