# \## SUMMARY

# 

# Veo 3.1 prefers prompts written like cinematic shot direction rather than simple image descriptions. The guide strongly emphasizes structured prompts built around cinematography, subject, action, context, and style. Detailed camera language, lighting, motion, environmental specificity, sound design, and emotional tone are repeatedly encouraged. The model responds well to professional filmmaking terminology, explicit sequencing, timestamp-based shot planning, and richly descriptive audiovisual direction. Audio prompting is treated as a first-class component, including dialogue, ambient sound, and sound effects. The guide consistently favors highly specific, cinematic, and technically directed prompts over short or generic instructions.
