# \## SUMMARY

# 

# Pika prefers concise, visually explicit prompts with strong emphasis on subject clarity, action, style direction, motion, and visual effects. The guide repeatedly encourages prompts that clearly describe what appears on screen, how it moves, and what visual aesthetic defines the scene. Stylization, transformations, dynamic motion, lighting effects, and cinematic atmosphere are heavily emphasized. The model performs best when prompts are compact, visually grounded, stylistically coherent, and focused on strong visual impact rather than lengthy narrative description.
